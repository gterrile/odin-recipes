Katsudon

About this recipe

The tonkatsu for the katsudon dish is prepared by dipping the cutlet in flour, followed by egg, then dipping in panko breadcrumbs, and deep-frying. Next, into a boiling broth of dashi, soy sauce and onions, the sliced tonkatsu and a beaten egg is cooked.

Ingredients

  • 1 tonkatsu
  • 1 bowl of white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of dashi
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the leftover katsudon into bite size pieces.
  2. Place the dashi, the sliced onions and the soy sauce in a small pan.
  3. Place the katsudon into the pan and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Beat 2 eggs and place them into the hot pan.
  5. Cook for 1 or 2 minutes to desired egg texture.
  6. Serve over a hot bowl of white rice.
  7. Top with the green part of the green onion.
